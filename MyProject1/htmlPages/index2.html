Physics notes


  • The weak nuclear force adjusts the relative numbers of neutrons and protons to produce stable nuclei.
  • The strong nuclear force holds protons and neutrons together in the atomic nuclei.
  • The strong nuclear force overcomes the repulsive electromagnetic force amond positive protons.

The weak nuclear force causes decay (things falling apart) while the strong nuclear force holds things together

  • Ionizing radiation strips electrons from atoms, turning the atoms into charged ions.
  • Radioactive nuclei are unstable. They emit ionizing radiation to become more stable.
  • Ionizing radiation is dangerous and bad! It's strong enough to strip away electrons
