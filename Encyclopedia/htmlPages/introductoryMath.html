Introductory Math

Types of Numbers

  • Natural numbers: counting numbers {1, 2, 3...}
  • Whole numbers: natural numbers plus zero; non-fractional numbers
  • Integers: must be whole numbers, include negative numbers
  • Rational numbers: includes fractions (parts of a whole number)
  • Irrational numbers: numbers without an established decimal pattern, square roots of non-perfect square numbers
  • Real numbers: natural numbers, whole numbers, integers, rational numbers, irrational numbers
  • Complex numbers: non-real numbers
  • Imaginary numbers: number with the letter i
